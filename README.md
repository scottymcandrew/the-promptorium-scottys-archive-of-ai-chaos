# The Promptorium: Scotty's Archive of AI Chaos

Welcome to my collection of AI prompts ranging from the absurdly hilarious to the genuinely useful. This is where I store the prompts I use for public speaking demos, experimentation, and general AI tomfoolery.

## What's Inside?

A chaotic mix of:
- **Satirical personas** that poke fun at corporate culture and tech industry stereotypes
- **Speaking event demos** designed to entertain and educate audiences
- **Creative experiments** that push AI in weird and wonderful directions
- **Serious, practical prompts** for actual work (yes, really)

Think of this as part comedy sketch archive, part professional toolkit, and part mad scientist's notebook.

Prompts are organized by:
